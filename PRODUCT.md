# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Wedding guests, friends, and family of Joao Vicente Murrieta Ratto and Vitalia Nataly Castillo Espinoza. 90% of them will access the invitation from their mobile devices.

## Product Purpose
A highly elegant, responsive, and romantic digital wedding invitation. It serves to inform guests of the wedding details (date, location, itinerary, dress code, gift registry, and bank accounts) and allows them to RSVP and add the event to their calendars.

## Brand Personality
- Romantic
- Modern
- Elegant & Refined
- Warm & Welcoming

## Anti-references
- SaaS-style layouts (generic landing page templates, identical cards in a grid)
- Loud color gradients, heavy shadows, or glassmorphism as decoration
- Text overflow on mobile screens
- Generic system fonts (Arial, Inter) in titles or display components

## Design Principles
1. **Stationery Elegance**: Recreate the tactile feel of high-end physical wedding stationery using the Crema, Navy, and Sage Green color palette with refined Serif and Calligraphy typography.
2. **Frictionless Actionability**: Provide instant utilities like "Copy" buttons for bank details, dropdowns for Calendar integration, and a database-backed RSVP form, minimizing user effort on mobile.
3. **Mobile-First Fluids**: Prioritize fluid sizing (`clamp()` for typography and margins) to ensure the mobile experience is perfectly polished and feels premium.
4. **Soft Entrance Choreography**: Implement scroll-triggered reveals with custom easing (`cubic-bezier(0.25, 1, 0.5, 1)`) to make the page elements unfold gracefully like a physical card.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliant text contrast (Azul Navy `#0D1B34` text on Crema `#F2EBDD` background holds a high contrast ratio).
- Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to disable or simplify entrance transitions for motion-sensitive users.
- Large and friendly touch targets (min 48x48px) for buttons.
